Privacy Policy

App: AgentFlow Pro
Effective date: January 30, 2026

Summary

  • AgentFlow Pro is designed to work offline-first.
  • Your property listings (text and photos) are stored locally on your device by default.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Subscriptions are handled by Apple; we do not receive your payment details.

1) Information We Collect

Data you create in the app (stored locally on your device):

  • Property listing details (e.g., title, type, price, size, location/zone, description, status).
  • Photos you attach to listings.
  • Optional internal notes you add for your own use.

Purchases / subscriptions:

  • Subscription purchase and renewal status is provided to the app by Apple (e.g., whether you have an active subscription).
  • We do not collect or store your credit card information. Payments are processed by Apple.

Diagnostics:

  • We may receive aggregated crash or performance information if you enable Apple’s diagnostics sharing on your device. This data is handled through Apple’s systems.

2) How We Use Information

  • To store and display your listings on your device.
  • To enable fast offline search and filtering.
  • To allow you to share selected listing content via the iOS share sheet (e.g., WhatsApp).
  • To determine feature access based on trial/subscription status (e.g., listing limits during trial).

3) Sharing of Information

AgentFlow Pro does not automatically send your listing data to our servers by default. Your data is shared only in these situations:

  • When you choose to share a listing (e.g., via WhatsApp, Messages, Email) using the iOS share sheet. The content you share is controlled by you.
  • With Apple for subscription verification and purchase handling.

4) Data Storage and Security

  • Your listings and photos are stored locally on your device.
  • Protect your device with a passcode/Face ID and keep iOS updated.
  • If you delete the app, local data may be removed from the device (unless backed up via iOS backup settings).

5) Permissions

  • Photos (optional): used to attach images to listings.
  • Notifications (optional): may be used for reminders or subscription/trial messaging, if implemented and enabled by you.

6) Children’s Privacy

AgentFlow Pro is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7) Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete listings at any time within the app.
  • You control what you share and with whom via the iOS share sheet.
  • You can manage subscription settings through your Apple ID / App Store settings.

8) Subscription Terms (Pricing & Renewal)

AgentFlow Pro offers a free trial and paid subscriptions. Pricing and availability may vary by region and may be adjusted by Apple. Current standard pricing:

  • 7 days free, then $29.99 monthly
  • $149.99 per 6 months
  • $249.99 per 12 months

Subscription auto-renews unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage and cancel anytime in your App Store account settings.
